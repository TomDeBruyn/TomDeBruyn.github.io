Mt. Leon

Mount Leon is a clothing brand designed by skiers for skiers, we find our ideas in nature.

We are a company located in the lowlands of Belgium, we provide outdoor sporters, from all over the world, with stylish clothing for sleeping, free time, work time and even for meetings. Our designs represent situations that will be familiar for every outdoor sporter.
